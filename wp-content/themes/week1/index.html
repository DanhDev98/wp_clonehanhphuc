Our Learning Pathway

Primary Years Programme

The IB PYP nurtures young learners by encouraging curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning. Through hands-on experiences and inquiry-based lessons, students build a strong foundation in academics while developing essential life skills.

Learn more

Grade 1 - Grade 5

Middle Years Programme

The IB MYP prepares students for future challenges by fostering critical thinking, interdisciplinary learning, and global awareness. It encourages students to make real-world connections and grow into well-rounded individuals.

Learn more

Grade 6 - Grade 10

Diploma Programme

The IB DP is a rigorous, globally recognized program that equips students with the knowledge and skills needed for university and beyond. With a focus on academic excellence and personal growth, the DP empowers students to succeed in a competitive world.

SCHOOL SPOTLIGHTS
THE BENEFITS OF LEARNING IN AN IB CONTINUUM SCHOOL
In our classrooms, you’ll find students working collaboratively on projects, conducting experiments, and engaging in discussions that challenge their assumptions and broaden their perspectives. Teachers act as facilitators, guiding students through the inquiry process and helping them to develop the skills necessary to become lifelong learners.
HOW SPORTS SHAPE STUDENT LEADERS
The CCA program at SNA Saigon South Campus exemplifies how sports can be a powerful tool for developing student leaders. By fostering teamwork, discipline, resilience, strategic thinking, and a sense of responsibility, the program equips students with the skills and mindset necessary to excel in leadership roles both in school and beyond.
ENSURING HEALTH AND WELLNESS WITH NUTRITIOUS MEALS AT SNA
The nutritious meals program at SNA Saigon South Campus has a profound positive impact on student life. By providing balanced meals, the program enhances academic performance, enabling students to focus better, retain information more effectively, and excel in their studies.
